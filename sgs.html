Get Started.

The rules are about to change...

We offer two basic services for your sneaker grading needs:

Green Label Grading (for wearable shoes):

Step 1: You fill in our short form, pay the grading fee ($24.99) + forward shipping fee ($14.99), clean your shoes and ship them to our closest location (sans box) - either in California or New York. *International users - contact us first.*
Step 2: We receive your shoes and run them through our meticulous 15 point inspection process with wearability in mind.
Step 3: We give your shoes spring loaded shoe trees and place them in vacuum-sealed biodegradable plastic bag.
Step 4: You tell us where to send 'em and we ship them out.

Red Label Grading (for non-wearable shoes):

Step 1: You fill in our short form, pay the fee ($74.99) + forward shipping fee ($24.99) and ship them to our closest location - either in California or New York. *International users - contact us first.*
Step 2: We receive your shoes and run them through our meticulous 20 point inspection process. We have THREE different experts inspect your shoes to ensure a unanimous grade.
Step 3: We give your shoes spring loaded shoe trees and position them in a hologram-sealed crystal-clear acrylic stackable display box - perfect for display.
Step 4: You tell us where to send 'em and we ship them out.


Get Graded.





Keep in mind:

1. We don't grade dirty shoes (accurately).

Nobody wants to receive a pair of dirty shoes, ever. We know cleaning shoes is a pain and is time consuming, but we need you to put your best foot forward. Any dirt we see - we're just going to assume it's stained and grade it accordingly.

2.We don't grade boxes.

We know they're neat and they look cool and add another dimension to your collection, but they're just too difficult to grade.
*If we receive a box, we won't grade them and will only send them back with a $10 fee. Seriously.*

3. We don't grade counterfeit shoes.

We know the market for fakes is huge, but it is something we fundamentally disagree with and simply will not support. If we notice you've submitted too many fake requests - you will be banned from our services.
